Company Overview

A disciplined platform for polymer supply

Sree Raviteja Polymers Limited (SRPL) has established itself as a trusted name in the polymer industry. With over three decades of expertise, the company has built a reputation for delivering on its promises, ensuring consistent supply and quality, and maintaining strong ethical business practices. Long-standing relationships with leading petrochemical corporations ensure uninterrupted supply and a competitive advantage.

By consistently meeting commitments, SRPL has earned a loyal customer base across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, serving the packaging, automotive, consumer goods, construction and agricultural industries. Today SRPL stands as a symbol of integrity, reliability and excellence in the polymer sector — backed by the Mantralaya Group's 35+ years in the industry.
